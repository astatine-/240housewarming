Detailed Driving Directions

  1. Reach RMZ Galleria Mall Yelahanka whichever way you want to

    Recommended: Coming from Hebbal, go on the main Airport road flyover, take the first exit ("the Coffee Day exit") and a Google guided U turn

  2. From RMZ Galleria Mall (also known as the old Yelahanka Traffic Police junction), take Doddaballapur Road
  3. The first 1.2 KM from there has construction going on and traffic might be a little slow
  4. Once you cross this you are just 12 minutes away
  5. At the 2.4 KM mark, you will pass Puttenahalli Lake on your left. Bengaluru's only in-city bird sanctuary!
  6. From 3.3 to 4.3 KMs you will pass the CRPF campus. A hat-tip to them, if you will
  7. At 4.75 KM you will have Shell petrol on your right
  8. At 5.75 KM you will pass Avalahalli Lake
  9. 9.63 KM brings you to Rajanakunte town (also the junction to go to the airport directly)
  10. At 10.50KM you pass Angsana Resorts
  11. If you are feeling that you may be lost and it can't be this far from the city, ignore that feeling
  12. At 13.50KM you will see a board saying Toll Booth 500m ahead. Slow down completely
  13. Immediately after this sign you will see a rather plain board on your left saying Citrus Earth Villas. Take a left, down the slope
  14. Pass the gate and ask for 240 ... we are a few houses down the road. You won't miss it :-)
  15. You can take a right turn immediately into a road where you can park
  16. Call Amita @ 96322 64270 if you need any help reaching
